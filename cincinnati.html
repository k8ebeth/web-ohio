Fun facts about Cincinnati

The Cincinnati Reds were the first professsional baseball team. The first city to publish greeting cards. The first city to establish a fire department and fireman's pole. University of Cincinnati was the first University.

Home of the famous Cincinnati Chili

The first heat-lung machine that made open heart surgery possible. The first city to have a licensed Public television station. (Hartness, 2023)(Fun Facts, 2014)
