Fun facts about Dayton

Dayton Ohio is where many important things were invented; the pop top can, and the ice cube tray. Electric ignition system for automobiles. The cash register, and the stepladder.

The home to the National US Air Force Museum

The birthplace of modern aviation. The Wright brothers invented the airplane. Dayton was founded on April fools day. The police department wrote the first speeding ticket. Dayton was founded before Ohio. (Hartness, 2023)(8 Fun Facts about Dayton, Ohio | Oberer Homes, 2022)
